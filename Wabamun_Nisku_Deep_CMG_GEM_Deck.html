Analytical Model References

  • Plume radius (sharp-interface) — Nordbotten, Celia & Bachu (2005). Transport in Porous Media, 58(3): 339–360. r²(t) = M(t) / (ρ_CO₂ · π · H · φ · (1 – S_wi – S_gr)).
  • Gravity-override tongue — Nordbotten & Celia (2006). JFM, 561: 307–327. Self-similar dimensionless solution.
  • Pressure buildup (radial) — Theis (1935). ΔP(r,t) = (Q·μ)/(4πkH) · W(u), with u = r²·φ·μ·c_t /(4kt). E₁(u) computed by Cody & Thacher rational approximation.
  • CO₂ solubility in brine — Spycher & Pruess (2005) GCA + Li & Nghiem (1986) salting-out correction.
  • Class VI / D065 — 40 CFR §146.81–95 (UIC Class VI); AER Directive 065 + Appendix P (MMV).
